#Property
Character: **W**
Thermodynamic Work is [[Internal Energy|Energy]] being transported over system boundaries that is not [[Heat (transfer)|heat]].

---

### Examples

#### Mechanical work
$$\int_{x_1}^{x_2}{Fdx} $$

#### Volume Work
$$F = p *A$$

#### Turbine Work
$$\dot{w} = \dot{m}(h_1-h_2)$$

#### Compressor / Pump Work
