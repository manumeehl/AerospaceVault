[[Inductor|Inductors]] connected in series have the following properties: 
$$
i_1(t) = i_2(t) = \ ... \ = i_n(t) = i(t)
$$
$$
v(t) = \sum_{\nu = 1}^n v_\nu(t)
$$
$$
L_{tot} = \sum_{\nu = 1}^n L_{\nu}
$$
This is analogously to the [[Resistor Series Connection]].
